# 08 - Referrals and leaderboards

## Referral MoA contests

**Straps, `#general`, Feb 9 and Feb 12 2026:** Referral contests reward **Marks of Ascendance** for bringing players. Links to `ascendanteq.com/referrals`. Framed as word-of-mouth growth for a new server.

That same period carried official "donations are gifts, no exchange" language in `#server-support`.

**Straps, `#server-support`, Feb 20 2026:** Donations described as voluntary gifts for hosting/infrastructure/tooling with **no goods or services in exchange**. Character name in Ko-fi message optional.

Referrals are not USD. They still convert social capital into premium currency that buys power/convenience (bags, buffs, ports). That blurs the non-commercial story.

## Feb 20: operator sending contest MoAs in public

Late on Feb 19 / early Feb 20 2026 in `#general`, a player asked Straps whether referral-contest MoAs had gone out. Straps replied on-record:

- **"Some, but not all - was waiti g on Names - but just saw you DM'd me so I'll send yours here in a minute"** (message `1474216329366999206`)
- **"thanks for all the referrals!"** (message `1474216457876279407`)

That is not a website rules page. It is the operator **manually mailing Marks** for a referral program while the same week’s `#server-support` post still says donations are gifts with **no goods or services in exchange**.

In the same thread, another player joked about MoAs for being helpful to newbies. **Ali (Kalioptra)** responded: **"Oh, are you handing out Marks for being helpful? I look forward to checking my parcels!"** Staff treated discretionary MoA drops as normal, not a policy violation.

Player handles from that pile-on stay out of the public quote file per redaction rules. Staff permalinks are in [data/discord-quotes-public.md](data/discord-quotes-public.md) under `08-referrals-leaderboards`.

## MoA vs other progression items (scope note)

This investigation tracked **Marks of Ascendance** because those are what referrals, Ko-fi optics, leaderboard buffs, and Straps’s staff MoA capture tie to **support and growth incentives**.

**Not seen in evidence as donor/referral fulfillment:**

| Item | Role in game | Operator → player gifts? |
|------|--------------|---------------------------|
| **Marks of Ascendance (MoA)** | Premium currency (bags, ports, buffs, Valeth unlocks) | **Yes** - referral contests, staff sends, Ko-fi-linked patterns (ch [05](05-donations-moa-kofi.md)) |
| **Illegible Tomes** | Mob drops → Guild Lobby Insights → chosen AAs | **No** - Straps patch note describes farming only ([quote corpus](data/discord-quotes-public.md)) |
| **AA / Insights** | Progression currency from tomes | **No** - in-game earn path only |
| **Shards of Ascendant Power** | Tradeable world-drop item (bazaar OOC) | **No** - player economy only; no staff mail or referral grant located |

Do not conflate a **tome/AA revamp announcement** with **MoA-for-support**. They are different systems. The public record supports **MoA** as the thing operators hand out for referrals and helpfulness jokes; we have **not** found parallel evidence for mailing tomes, AA ranks, or Shards to donors or referrers.
