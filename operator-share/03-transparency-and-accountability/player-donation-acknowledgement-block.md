## Full acknowledgement (checkbox before external donate link)

> **Ascendant Donation Transparency Acknowledgement**

>
> 1. **Voluntary support.** Donations are **optional** and **not required** to play. The operator states **no exclusive in-game items** are sold for donations.

>

>
> 4. **My expectations.** I donate understanding that **maintenance and uptime** depend partly on **home broadband reliability**, not only datacenter SLAs - unless the operator publishes a updated cost breakdown saying otherwise.
>
> 5. **Character names.** If I include a character name on Ko-fi, I have read how the operator **uses** that information (thank-you only vs internal tracking) - or I donate **without** a character name.
>
> 6. **Scope.** This is **transparency for informed giving**, not an accusation of fraud or theft. I may still choose to support the community on my own terms.
>
> ☐ I have read and understand the above before supporting Ascendant financially.
