# TR-030: Transparency topics split across briefs - needs single rollout owner

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|

## Summary

Transparency findings intentionally **span four technical briefs** (login, hosting, donations, patcher). **03-transparency-and-accountability** is the **rollout index** - not a fifth duplicate of DT/LS/RH/PC IDs. Operators should assign **one owner** for player-facing publication and track remediation via **TR-xxx** plus underlying finding IDs.

## Example

| If player asks… | Technical brief | Player notice |
|-----------------|-----------------|---------------|
| Where does my password go? | 01 LS-015 | login notice |
| Where does my character live? | 02 RH-011 | hosting notice |
| What does my donation fund? | 03 DT-010 | donation notice |
| What did patcher install? | 04 PC-002 | client notice |

**03** answers: “Where do I read all of this on **your** official site?” (TR-001 hub + this index).
