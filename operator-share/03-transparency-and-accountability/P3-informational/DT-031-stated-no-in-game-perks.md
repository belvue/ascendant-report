# DT-031: Page states no in-game perks for donations (clarity of intent)

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Priority** | P3 - Informational |
| **Status** | Confirmed (Server Support + Discord `#server-support` policy text) |
| **Component** | Governance - positive disclosure |
| **CWE** | N/A |

## Summary

Ascendant’s Server Support page and pinned Discord **Server Donations / Support** policy **clearly state** donations are **voluntary**, **not required to play**, and provide **no exclusive in-game items** or commercial purchase framing. This is **good practice** and should be **preserved** while improving **cost and receipt transparency** (DT-001, DT-010, DT-011).

## Example

**Stated on Server Support and Discord policy (observed):**

- “Ascendant EQ is **not a commercial service** and donations are **not a purchase**.”
- “**No exclusive in-game items** are offered in exchange for donations.”
- “Donations are **never required** to play or progress.”
- “Donations are treated as **personal gifts** … **no goods or services** are provided in exchange.”
- Appeals may list **hosting, infrastructure, maintenance, tooling, and development** as cost areas - separate from perks policy.

## Player scenario

**Context:** Player worried about pay-to-win before donating.

**What happens:**

1. Official page denies **pay-to-win** exchange - credible baseline.
2. Separate question remains: **what costs** donations cover (**DT-001**, **PF-001**) and **how runway is calculated** (**DT-010**).

**Not an exploit:** Positive note - reduces pay-to-win concern; does not alone satisfy hosting-cost or funding-model transparency.

## Implications

- **Keep** this language when revising runway and Ko-fi copy.
- Transparency work should **add** cost/receipt detail - not remove no-perks clarity.

## Remediation

- No change required to no-perks statements; **retain prominently** after DT-010/011 and **05** funding-model updates.

## Verification

- Post-remediation Server Support still includes **no pay-to-win** bullets above the fold.
