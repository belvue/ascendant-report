# Player notice - Ascendant donations & Server Support (v1)

**Audience:** Players considering Ko-fi or Server Support donations  
**Date:** 2026-06-13  
**Tone:** Informed giving - **not** a fraud accusation

---

## Why this notice exists

Many players donate because they want Ascendant **online, maintained, and stable**. The official **Server Support** page promises transparency about how donations cover costs and shows a **“Server Runway”** bar. This notice explains **what is publicly verifiable today** and **what donors may reasonably want clarified** - without claiming criminal intent.

**Companion (hosting facts):** [02 player hosting notice](../02-residential-host-brief/player-hosting-notice-v1.md) - where the game world runs (residential vs OVH).

---

## What donations are for (official language)

Ascendant asks for **voluntary** support to help with **hosting, operating, maintenance, tooling, and development**. The page states clearly:

- **No pay-to-win** - no exclusive in-game items for donating
- **Not required** to play or progress
- **Personal gifts** - not a purchase of goods or services

That **clarity of intent is good** and should stay.

---

## What many donors assume vs what probes show

| Donor assumption | Public evidence today |
|----------------|----------------------|
| Donations fund **datacenter game hosting** and **uptime** | **Game world** runs on **residential home broadband**; **website/CDN** on **OVH** - split **not explained** on Server Support |
| Runway bar = **honest months of server bills covered** | Bar shows **one blended “months remaining”** number - **no breakdown** of OVH vs home game vs dev time |
| “Making open” how much donations cover costs | **Runway months** shown; **totals received by channel** (e.g. Ko-fi) **not published** on the page |
| Staff know my character if I put it in Ko-fi | **Optional character name** invited - **no published policy** on how names are used |

**OVH spending for web/CDN is real.** The gap is **unsplit, incomplete math** - not “donations are fake.”

---

## What this notice does not claim

- That operators **stole** donation money
- That donations are **legally fraudulent**
- That donors **always** receive hidden in-game benefits
- An **EQEmu Foundation ruling** on Server Operator Terms (**review was requested publicly; outcome not captured here**)

This is about **informed consent for givers** who expect **maintenance and uptime transparency**.

---

## What would help donors (operator actions)

1. **Cost categories** - OVH web/CDN, login/portal (if billed), residential game (marginal note), dev/tooling (optional line).
2. **Runway math on-page** - reserve, monthly burn, **last updated**, and **what’s included** in burn.
3. **Receipt summary** - Ko-fi totals (30-day / 12-month bands OK), other channels listed or “Ko-fi only.”
4. **Same story** on Ko-fi and Server Support - one topology, one formula.
5. **Character names** - publish whether they’re thank-you only, never used for perks (**they shouldn’t be**).
