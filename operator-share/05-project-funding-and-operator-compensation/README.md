# Project funding & operator compensation - findings archive

**Product:** Ascendant EQ community funding (Server Support, Ko-fi, runway API)  
**Assessment date:** 2026-06-13 (public materials); Ko-fi observations through 2026-06-16  
**Scope:** Whether contributors can understand **how donated funds may be allocated** - categories, reserves, capital vs operating costs, and compensation policy

This is **not** a misconduct investigation. Reserves, development time, and steward labor are **legitimate** project expenses when disclosed.

Donation **receipt totals**, Ko-fi feed behavior, trust hub, and informed-consent UX live in **[03-transparency-and-accountability](../03-transparency-and-accountability/README.md)** (DT-xxx, KF-xxx).
