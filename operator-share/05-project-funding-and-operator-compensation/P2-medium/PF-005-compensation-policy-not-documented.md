# PF-005: Compensation policy for operators, devs, and stewards not published

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Priority** | P2 - Medium |
| **Status** | Confirmed (no public compensation policy located) |
| **Component** | Governance - labor / compensation |
| **CWE** | N/A (disclosure) |

## Summary

The project has **operators, developers, GMs, stewards, and moderators** (public steward program). **No published policy** states whether any role receives **money, goods, or in-game privileges** funded by community donations.

**Compensation can be a legitimate project expense.** This finding is only that **contributors cannot tell** from official sources whether it occurs or is prohibited.

## Example

**Observed:**

| Topic | Public documentation |
|-------|---------------------|
| No in-game perks for donors | **Yes** - DT-031 |
| Steward selection / voting | **Yes** - website steward page |
| Whether stewards/GMs/devs are **paid** from Ko-fi | **Not found** |
| Whether operators may **reimburse themselves** for hardware/ISP | **Not found** |

**Patch-note context (paraphrased):** Operator thanked donors for reducing personal **financial burden** alongside **time commitment** - acknowledges personal cost, not a compensation schedule.

## Player scenario

**Context:** Contributor asks Discord whether donations “pay the devs.”

**What happens:**

1. Official **no perk** policy applies to **donors**, not **staff**.
2. Without staff compensation policy, community speculates.
3. One paragraph (“all roles unpaid” or “stipend policy below”) closes the gap.

**Not claimed:** Secret payroll - **policy absence**.

## Implications

- Pairs with **PF-002**, **DT-031**, **KF-002** (economy review is separate from payroll).

## Remediation

1. Publish **compensation policy**: unpaid volunteer / reimbursement allowed / stipend bands - pick one and date it.
2. State whether **in-game GM powers** are ever tied to financial support (expected: **no**).
3. If compensation is **$0**, say so explicitly - reduces rumor surface.

## Verification

- Server Support includes **Staff & compensation** bullet.
- Policy covers **operators, contractors, stewards, GMs** at high level without naming individuals.
